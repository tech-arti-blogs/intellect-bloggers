BIO-INFORMATICS

7 million people die every year due to cancer. By next year, this will become 16 million. Researchers identified about 200 varieties of cancer. Cancer is caused because of rapid cell growth in the body which leads to death. Countries like Asia, South America, Africa and Central America counts for 70% of the cancer related deaths in the overall world population. On the other hands, bioinformation is an inter-disciplinary field which includes Computer Science, Mathematics and other. Huge biological data can be stored, analysed and manipulated in using bioinformatics. Advanced tools and effective algorithms are helpful in analysing these biological data. Thus, in this some of the databases that provide cancer related data were discussed.
