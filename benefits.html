E-learning is a new method of learning which is attracted by many people in the present era. E-learning has many advantages which attracts the crowd.

 Personalization:

The materials for learning are chosen directly by the learners according to their needs. But in traditional learning the learning materials are not chosen by students themselves.

 Efficiency:

In E-learning, there are enormous materials for the learners, which makes them more efficient and stronger in that particular concept.

 Cost – Effective:

In E-learning, students can learn anything from anywhere. Cost of travelling and buying stationaries like pen, notebook, etc… will be reduced. Another important cost – effectiveness is, students can learn even from their homes, there is no need of any particular institutions.

 Elimination of fear:

In traditional learning, a student may fear/hinder for asking his/her doubts. But in E-learning, the discussion forums eliminate that fear.

 Flexibility:

The learners are more flexible with E-learning than traditional learning. There is a choice for learners in choosing the time and place for learning.

 Communication:

E-learning increases the chances of communication with others. This interaction helps them to know other’s point of view. This interactivity will make the learning process easier.

 Individuality:

Unlike traditional learning in which all the students are insisted to focus on the same thing. In E-learning, every individual student has their option such as whether to focus on the entire course or to focus on the certain part of the course content.

 Self – Pacing:

Every student’s grasping power differs. In E-learning, each student takes up the course according to his convenience.

 Eliminates Scarcity:

E-learning eliminates the scarcity of teachers/instructors. One instructor is enough to teach for as much students as possible.
