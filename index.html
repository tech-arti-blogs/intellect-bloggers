BIG DATA

Title description, March 2020

Data is generated day by day enormously in terabytes. These data are of various forms and not similar in nature. This kind of large data sets referred to as big data. Huge volumes of both structural and unstructured data are present in the world. There were many technologies evolved such as Internet of Things, cloud computing, and so on. One of the techniques that stores huge data is big data. The data which is stored has to analysed which is a challenging part. Thus, in this review, the architecture of big data is illustrated. Following that, in materials and methods detailed survey of tools used for big data process is present. After which, open research issues associated with Big data in various fields are represented.

E-LEARNING

Title description, JUNE 2020

The technological advancements of the present world paves way to the modern teaching – learning process called E-learning. E-learning has become an in-demand learning approach in the past few years. The concept of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) is utilized for E-learning approach. E-learning facilities the students/learners in many ways such as time, place etc… which makes it more popular. Many research findings prove that E-learning is more effective. This paper explores the evolution period of E-learning the architecture behind it, the tools it the types it contains and also discusses the benefits as well as the drawbacks.

CYBER CRIME & CYBER SECURITY

Title description, JANUARY 2020

In this digital world, there isn’t civil war exists but exists a cyber war. Cybercrime is a criminal activity which takes our data far away from privacy. Cybercrime is done with computers and networks with the aim of attaining any profit. This crime is done by various methods like hacking, fraud, spreading of virus and others. Cyber security plays a vital role in opposing cybercrime. Cyber security protects our data from many criminal activities such as hacking, piracy and others. Cyber security also adopts various techniques for attacking. In this paper both cybercrime and cyber security techniques are discussed. Some detailed statistical analysis was also made.
