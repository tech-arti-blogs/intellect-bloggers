E- learning has many deviations from conventional learning technique. E-learning also possess some bad impacts on the society.

 Lack of interaction:

E-learning may result in lack of direct interaction with other students.

 Lack of practical knowledge:

Even though the concept is well explained in E-learning, the chances of practical experimentation of those concepts are less.

 Piracy:

E-learning may sometimes pave way to plagiarism of content and piracy. This may mislead the selection process if any.

 Bad conduct:

In E-learning, it is difficult to control any kind of bad conducts such as cheating.

 Interest:

In traditional way of learning, the students are made to sit and listen in particular time and place which may make them gain knowledge. But in E-learning, it is only their self-interest to spare their time which can have a negative effect.

 Website Congestion:

If any particular tutorial is watched more this may lead to website congestion.

 Content non-reliability:

The content in the websites may not be reliable. The learners must be careful with the sources of the content.

 Cost of Setup:

Though E-learning is cost-effective, the cost of setup of environment with equipment like projections, laptops, etc…

 Technology dependency:

Still there are students who do not opt with good internet connectivity and devices like desktops, laptops. Because of that the learning process is tedious.

 Health Issues:

While learning online, the students are supposed to view laptops or any other devices. Which may cause damage to their eyes. As well as many other problems such as neck strain etc… can occur.
