E-LEARNING

The evolution of E-learning has become a greatest advantage in education sector. E-learning is a large-scale method for training learners all over the world . The students are more convenient while learning online because they can choose their time and place of learning. In current scenario, the students/learners have two methodologies for learning which conventional and online learning. Conventional learning is more dependable on teachers but online learning is more dependable on learners and the learner’s self-interest and determination drives them completely. The advancements of multimedia and reachability of internet to the mass population has made this E-learning technology more popular. E-learning should motivate people to learn more and more. Thus, for the beginners who wants to know about E-learning, this paper provides a skeleton on the topic “E-learning” starting from its evolution until its benefits and drawbacks. This paper briefly describes the history, architecture and components, the common tools, the various types and finally the pros and cons.
