E-learning is a trending tool in learning process. To achieve the objectives, E-learning uses the telecommunication technology. Special training through theoretical and practical tools promotes the learning to next levels.

 Blog:

The term blog evolved from “web” and “log”. The blog entries can be viewed as journal style of entries in renewable chronological order. The contents of blog provide information or comments related to any particular subject.

 Social bookmarking:

Social bookmarking is a method in which sites they visit can be shared as links to others. So that the resources can be used by others too.

 RSS:

RSS feed publishers frequenting updated belongs/feeds. The user feeds to the RSS by giving links and the readers gets updated and view those feeds.

 Wiki:

A wiki is a source of information in which any user is allowed to modify the content without registration.

 Forums:

Forums are like discussion area in which the topics are open to public opinion and anyone can share their views towards it.

 Messaging Application:

Instant messaging application helps to communicate to other persons over a network. Applications such as Skype, Google Meet, etc…

 Text Chat:

Online chat rooms can be created using which others also invited to join the link via sharing of link.
