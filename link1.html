BIG DATA

Almost half the overall world’s population is available online. A census report of 2018 shows that the number of internet users in 2018 is 4.021 billion and this shows increases each year by 7 percent. The data occupies the web are growing in exponential fashion [8]. Thus, the volume of large data sets both structured and unstructured data are referred to as big data [2]. And the analysis of these large data sets is known as big data analytics.

NIST defined big data characteristics are: Volume, Velocity, Variety and Variability.

Volume – Describes size of the dataset.

Variety – Describes data from multiple repositories, domains or types.

Velocity – Describes rate of data flow.

Variability – Describes the change in other characteristics.
